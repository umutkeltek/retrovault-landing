RetroVault Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

What RetroVault stores

RetroVault stores your local app settings, declared age bracket, import records, save files, and purchase state on your device. No ROMs are included, and RetroVault does not provide game downloads.

Game files

You choose files from your own device or cloud storage provider through iOS import flows. RetroVault does not upload your legally owned game files to a RetroVault server.

Diagnostics and purchases

If you opt into analytics, RetroVault may record unlinked diagnostic and product-interaction events to improve reliability. Purchase processing is handled by Apple through the App Store.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact retrovault@keltek.ai.
