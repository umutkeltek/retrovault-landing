RetroVault Help

Support response target: within 48 hours after launch.

Importing files

Use the iOS Files app, Share Sheet, or iPad drag and drop to import legally owned files. RetroVault does not include or download games for you.

Purchases

Use App Store purchase history to manage subscriptions, trials, cancellation, and refunds. The app includes Restore Purchases in Settings.
