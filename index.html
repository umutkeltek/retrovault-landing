FAQ

Clear answers before launch.

Does RetroVault include games?

No. No ROMs are included. You import legally owned files yourself through iOS-supported flows.

What is free?

The base play loop and battery saves are free. Pro adds convenience tools such as save states, rewind, fast-forward, sync, controller profiles, and premium shaders.

Why publish a source offer?

RetroVault uses mGBA under MPL-2.0. The public source-offer fork keeps covered source available for App Review and users.

When is the App Store launch?

The App Store listing is pending final external checks, including policy URLs, screenshots, product approvals, and TestFlight review.
